Common Questions

Getting a room started

How do I start a game?

Tap Create Room, enter your name, choose the room settings, then share the room code with the people playing with you.

How do friends join?

Friends tap Join Room, enter the room code, and add their name. The host starts the round once everyone is in the lobby.

Do players need accounts?

No. SO You! is designed for quick rooms with names and room codes, so players can join without creating an account.

What are decoys?

Decoys are extra fake answers mixed into the room. They make guessing harder and add a little chaos to each reveal.

Why did my room disconnect?

Rooms need a stable internet connection. If a room stalls, have the host return to the lobby or create a new room and invite everyone back.

How do I save a personality card?

After the room results are available, unlock the personality cards and use the save/share controls shown in the app.
